About Us

Bridging the Gap Between Farmers & Buyers

At Assured Contract Farming (ACF), we believe in empowering farmers and providing buyers with the freshest organic produce directly from the source. Our platform eliminates middlemen, ensuring fair prices for farmers and premium quality for consumers.

With over a decade of experience in agricultural trade, we have built a network of 500+ verified farmers and 1000+ happy buyers across Maharashtra.
